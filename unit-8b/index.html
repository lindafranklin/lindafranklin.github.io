A Little Bit About Me

There is nothing I enjoy more (of course, outside of helping you with your digital marketing challenges!) than enjoying the outdoors with my big, shaggy German shepherd, Sam. We have hiked just about every trail of the Manassas Battlefield, although I believe he would be prefer to stay on the couch!

If I’m not hiking with Sam, you can find me competing in 5ks, 10ks and the occasional marathon. (Well, OK, I did one marathon, the Marine Corp Marathon. And it was such an amazing experience that I will definitely add another to my list before too long!)

I’ve been told that I simply can’t run for fun. I always need to have a goal.

That is true. It definitely reflects my innate personality of planning and strategizing to meet the end goal. I believe that is why I have excel in the areas of project and web management. I love to create efficient processes, timely plans, and research best practices to create the best end goal possible.
